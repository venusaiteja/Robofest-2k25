About the Event

Robo Football League is a competitive robotics event where teams design, build, and control robots to play football in a dynamic arena. Participants can compete in Singles or Doubles categories, each requiring teamwork, engineering skill, and strategic gameplay.

Event Categories

Singles

• 3 members per team
• 1 robot per team

Doubles

• 6 members per team
• 2 robots (Goalkeeper + Striker)
